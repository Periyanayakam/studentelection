About ElectVote Platform

ElectVote is a state-of-the-art secure online voting portal built explicitly for academic representation, student councils, and department representatives.

Our platform eliminates paper ballots, long queues, and physical polling booths while upholding the absolute confidentiality of every voter. Every voter registration goes through verification steps, and vote logs are encrypted to ensure complete reliability.

Core Features

Designed For Transparency and Trust

Our platform guarantees a fair, private, and authenticated election experience.

End-to-End Encryption

Every ballot cast is cryptographically processed to ensure that votes cannot be altered or traceably linked to individuals.

Verified Registrations

Automatic validation checks ensure that only eligible voters with valid Student and Voter ID credentials can access ballots.
